MSA Architects has worked with Xavier University for over a decade. Starting from an overall campus master plan in 2005, our team recently worked on the transformation of Alter Hall, which has become the most iconic building on campus today.

Winner of numberous national awards, Alter Hall has become the new baseline for universities nationwide
