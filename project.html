from decline to destination

The Empower Group was in need of a new space to house their newly abundant design team.

The group wanted to remain within one of the City’s ‘hot spots’ as the company’s culture relies heavily on the upbeat tempo and innovative atmosphere of these areas. While scouring the area for a space large enough to accommodate everyone, the MSA Architects design team developed a program that includes the confidence of Empower Group without relinquishing either their personality.
